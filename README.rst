================
Chain Calculator
================

A simple chain calculator for the command line.

Examples::

    $ chain_calc 4 plus 1 minus 2 times 3 by 9
    1

    $ chain_calc -v 4 plus 1 minus 2 times 3 by 9
    4 + 1 = 5
    5 - 2 = 3
    3 * 3 = 9
    9 / 9 = 1
    1
